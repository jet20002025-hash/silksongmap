Essential Boss Fights

Moss Mother - First Major Boss

📍 Moss Grotto - Eastern Chamber | Difficulty: ★★☆☆☆

280 HP 2 Damage 3:30 Avg Time

The first major test in Silksong. Learn poison patterns and platform mobility.

💡 Pro Tips:

  • • Stay mobile - poison floor deals damage over time
  • • Watch for 1.5-second inhale before poison spray
  • • Kill moss creepers for silk refills

Lace - The Persistent Rival

📍 Multiple Encounters | Difficulty: ★★★★☆

450+ HP 3 Damage 5:45 Avg Time

Adaptive AI that learns from your strategies. Changes moveset based on your playstyle.

💡 Pro Tips:

  • • Vary your approach - she adapts to repeated strategies
  • • Golden flash indicates parryable attacks
  • • Stay mid-range to limit her options

Steel Assassin Sharpe

📍 Hunter's March - Colosseum | Difficulty: ★★★★★

600 HP 4 Damage 8:20 Avg Time

The ultimate skill test. Lightning-fast attacks with minimal telegraphs.

💡 Pro Tips:

  • • Master parrying - essential for survival
  • • Learn audio cues for attack prediction
  • • Save silk for emergency healing

Essential Controls
