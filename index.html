Complete Boss Strategies

Essential Boss Fights

Moss Mother - First Major Boss

📍 Moss Grotto - Eastern Chamber | Difficulty: ★★☆☆☆

280 HP 2 Damage 3:30 Avg Time

The first major test in Silksong. Learn poison patterns and platform mobility.

💡 Pro Tips:

  • • Stay mobile - poison floor deals damage over time
  • • Watch for 1.5-second inhale before poison spray
  • • Kill moss creepers for silk refills

Lace - The Persistent Rival

📍 Multiple Encounters | Difficulty: ★★★★☆

450+ HP 3 Damage 5:45 Avg Time

Adaptive AI that learns from your strategies. Changes moveset based on your playstyle.

💡 Pro Tips:

  • • Vary your approach - she adapts to repeated strategies
  • • Golden flash indicates parryable attacks
  • • Stay mid-range to limit her options

Steel Assassin Sharpe

📍 Hunter's March - Colosseum | Difficulty: ★★★★★

600 HP 4 Damage 8:20 Avg Time

The ultimate skill test. Lightning-fast attacks with minimal telegraphs.

💡 Pro Tips:

  • • Master parrying - essential for survival
  • • Learn audio cues for attack prediction
  • • Save silk for emergency healing

The Map Master

Shakra is Pharloom's resident cartographer, providing essential maps and navigation tools for your journey through the kingdom.

She can be found in various locations throughout your adventure, offering maps for different areas at varying costs.

Map Prices

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

🎮 Game Basics

When was Hollow Knight: Silksong released?

Hollow Knight: Silksong was officially released on September 4, 2025, available on multiple platforms.

How big is the Silksong map?

The Silksong map is significantly larger than the original Hollow Knight, spanning 6-8 major areas with Greymoor as the central hub.

How many bosses are in Silksong?

Silksong contains 40+ bosses, including major area bosses, mini-bosses, and secret encounters.

Do I need to play the original Hollow Knight first?

While not required, we strongly recommend playing the original game first to better understand the world and character backgrounds.

⚔️ Gameplay & Strategy

How long is the game?

The game is approximately 15-20 hours long, depending on your exploration level and skill.

What are Hornet's new abilities?

Hornet has a completely new movement system including jumping, gliding, and a series of weapon skills with silk abilities.

How is gameplay different from Hollow Knight?

Silksong features Hornet as the protagonist with unique abilities including needle combat, silk movement, and different magic systems.

Will there be DLC?

Team Cherry hasn't announced DLC plans yet, but considering their free DLC support for the original game, Silksong will likely receive additional content updates.
